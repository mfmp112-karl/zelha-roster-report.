The bottom line

@jayjnrke is the one to contract first — the strongest gym engagement in the group (19%), the only creator whose fitness posts reliably beat their own feed, and already reposted four times.

@cialoziofitness is the breakout bet. Only 112 followers, yet its gym videos pull ~8,900 views per 1,000 followers — content travelling nine times its audience — and Zelha reposts it more than anyone (7×). Small, on-brand, high-ceiling.
