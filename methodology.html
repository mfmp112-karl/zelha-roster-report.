How this was measured

Everything here comes from public TikTok metrics, computed the same way for every account. Where a finding could be read as advice, it carries a significance verdict so a small sample can't masquerade as a trend.

Data source
yt-dlp flat enumeration of each public profile — views, likes, comments, shares, caption, date, duration. No logins, no paid APIs, no video downloads. 840 videos across the six accounts.
Gym relevance
A transparent keyword lexicon (English + Swahili/Sheng, plus spin/Zumba/class terms) tags each video as fitness or not, from its caption and hashtags. 533 of the videos qualified as gym-relevant.
Engagement rate
View-weighted likes + comments + shares as a percentage of views — size-independent, so it compares fairly across very different follower counts.
Views per 1k followers
Median gym-video views ÷ followers × 1,000. Surfaces content that travels beyond its existing audience — the clearest influencer signal.
Significance
Each “gym vs own feed” gap is tested with Welch's t-test on log(views). Verdicts: strong (p<0.01), moderate (p<0.05), or too early to tell. Small accounts almost always read “too early” — by design, not by fault.
Reposts
Captured from Zelha's logged-in “Reposts” tab (not exposed to logged-out visitors or the public API). Each repost links to the original creator — that's the proof-of-fit.
Follower counts
Read from each profile header (server-rendered, so public). Point-in-time on 23 Jul 2026; @__kama.u's header didn't render a count, so its per-1k figure is blank.

Honest limitations

Public metrics only. Watch-time, traffic sources and audience demographics are owner-only in TikTok Studio and can't be seen for anyone else — no tool can.
Small samples. @__kama.u (4 videos) and @cialoziofitness (17) are too small for statistical verdicts; treat their rates as directional, not settled.
Caption-based relevance. The gym filter reads captions and hashtags, not the video itself, so a wordless workout clip with an off-topic caption can be missed.
Point-in-time. Pulled 23 Jul 2026. Views and followers keep moving; re-run to refresh.
